Step-by-step tutorial — molecular dynamics, capillary filling of a carbon nanotube (CNT)

The system is a carbon nanotube in contact with a water reservoir. A moving wall present the evaporation of water molecules. Molecular dynamics simulations are performed with the code LAMMPS, atom visualisation is made with VMD, and data plotting with Xmgrace.

Step-by-step tutorial — molecular dynamics for absolute beginners

The system is a cube filled with water molecules (TIP4P/2005 model). Molecular dynamics simulations are performed with the code LAMMPS, atom visualisation is made with VMD, and data plotting with Xmgrace. The water is first equilibrated at ambient temperature and ambient pressure, then, basic quantities such as density, diffusion coefficient or viscosity are measured.
