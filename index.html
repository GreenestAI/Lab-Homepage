Step-by-step tutorial — molecular dynamics for absolute beginer

The system is a cube filled with water molecules (TIP4P/2005 model). Molecular dynamics simulations are performed with the code LAMMPS, atom visualisation is made with VMD, and data plotting with xmgrace. The water is first equilibrated at ambient temperature and ambient pressure, then, basic quantities such as density, diffusion coefficient or viscosity are measured.
