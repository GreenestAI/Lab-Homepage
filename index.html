Molecular dynamics: capillary filling of a carbon nanotube (how-to)

The simulation is made using LAMMPS. The system is a carbon nanotube (CNT) in a membrane, in contact with a reservoir of water molecules. A moving wall (piston) prevents the evaporation of water molecules. Atom visualisation is made with VMD, and data plotting with Xmgrace.

Molecular dynamics: viscosity measurement of bulk water (step-by-step tutorial)

The simulation is made using LAMMPS. The system is a cube of water molecules (TIP4P/2005 model). The water is first equilibrated at ambient temperature and ambient pressure, then, basic quantities such as density and viscosity are measured. Atom visualisation is made with VMD, and data plotting with Xmgrace.
