Prof. SungKu Heo presented “Nationwide techno-economic and environmental assessment of wastewater-derived hydrogen production for the transition to a hydrogen economy” at the conference of the Korean Society of Environmental Engineers (KSEE). The presentation focused on evaluating the feasibility of large-scale hydrogen production from wastewater resources across South Korea, with emphasis on integrated techno-economic analysis and life cycle environmental impacts to support a sustainable hydrogen economy.
