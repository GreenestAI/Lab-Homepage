Seonju Kim, an integrated BS/MS course student, presented a poster at the 2025 Fall Meeting of the Korean Society of Industrial and Engineering Chemistry (KSIEC), held on November 7, 2025, at EXCO, Daegu.
The poster presentation was titled “Development of an N₂O Soft-Sensing Algorithm Based on Water AI-Ready Data,” and focused on the development of a data-driven soft-sensing framework for N₂O emissions using water AI-ready datasets.
