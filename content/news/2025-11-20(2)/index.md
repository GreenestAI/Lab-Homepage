Prof. SungKu Heo presented “Autonomous operation of wastewater treatment plants based on digital twins: Trustworthy AI-driven optimal control and real-world validation” at the conference of the Korean Society of Environmental Engineers (KSEE). The presentation focused on the development of a digital twin framework integrated with trustworthy AI for autonomous process control, with emphasis on enhancing operational reliability, optimal performance, and practical implementation in full-scale wastewater treatment system.
