Prof. SungKu Heo was invited to present “AI Transformation in Atmospheric Aerosol: Trace Pollutants Softsensing, HVAC Control, Nationwide Monitoring” at Aorean association for particle and aerosol research (KAPAR), Korea. The presentation focused on state-of-tho-art of AI applications in atmospheric particle and aerosol research works and future direction of AI transformation (AX), including data-centric AI-driven trace pollutants softsensing, RL-driven HVAC autonomous control, multimodal AI-driven nationwide monitoring.
