SeonJu proudly represented our lab at KSEE, and we are thrilled to share that she received the [Conference Paper Award](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1jZOj6SfDbaI5x1aSEmxAbKMsSwdmqV7B/view?usp=drive_link) for her work, “A Multi-Objective Superstructure Optimization Framework for Regional Biogasification Facility Design Using Techno-Economic and Environmental Data.”

Her research presents a multi-objective superstructure optimization framework for designing regional biogasification facilities by integrating technical, economic, and environmental data. The proposed approach identifies optimal facility configurations tailored to regional organic waste characteristics while considering both economic feasibility and environmental performance. Congratulations, SeonJu! 🎉🏆
