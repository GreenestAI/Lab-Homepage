Dr. Jihoon Shin from the Gangwon Institute delivered an invited seminar on “Water Environment Management and AI Application.” During the seminar, he introduced recent advances in AI-driven technologies for water quality monitoring, environmental data analysis, and intelligent water resource management. The presentation highlighted how artificial intelligence and digital transformation are reshaping conventional approaches to environmental monitoring and decision-making processes in the water sector. The seminar also provided practical perspectives on integrating AI models with real-world environmental systems, including applications in predictive analysis, anomaly detection, and smart environmental management strategies. Students and researchers had the opportunity to discuss the future potential of AI-based environmental engineering technologies and their role in building more sustainable and resilient water management systems.
