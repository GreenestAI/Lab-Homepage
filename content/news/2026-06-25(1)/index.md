SeonJu delivered an oral presentation on “A Multi-Objective Superstructure Optimization Framework for Regional Biogasification Facility Design Using Techno-Economic and Environmental Data” at the Korea Society of Waste Management (KSWM), Korea.

The presentation focused on a multi-objective superstructure optimization framework for designing regional biogasification facilities. The proposed framework integrates techno-economic and environmental data to identify optimal facility configurations tailored to regional organic waste characteristics while balancing economic feasibility and environmental performance. 🎤🌱
